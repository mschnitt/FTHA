Parking Fee:
Although the park does not charge an entrance fee, they do charge a parking fee to park in the lot. Note that the park has no concessesions so a picnic lunch is recommended
Vehicle $6.00
Senior $5.00
Disabled $3.00
Bus (24 pssenger or less) $50.00
Bus (25 passenger or more) $125.00
   
Additional Fees for the Civil War Event:  
Ages 15 and older $20.00
Ages 6-14 (7 - 17) $10.00
Children 5 and under Free
Family Active Military Free
   
Tour Groups:
Fort tejon provides guided tours for both individuals and schools groups. Please call the fort at (661) 248-6692 for more information.
   
Camping:
Fort tejon has a group reservation campground behind the fort.
Campground Reservations: Go to Reserve California and search for Fort Tejon SHP
