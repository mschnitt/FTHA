MAP & DIRECTIONS
 
 

Fort Tejon is on Interstate 5, near the town of Lebec, about 36 miles south
of Bakersfield and 77 miles north of Los Angeles.

HOURS

The Fort Tejon State Historic Park grounds are open from sunrise to sunset every day.
The buildings are open from 8 am to 4 pm.
