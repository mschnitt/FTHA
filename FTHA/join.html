JOIN THE FTHA!
As a member of the Fort Tejon Historical Association you support Fort Tejon's interpretive programs and restoration of the historic fort. You also receive free admission to FTHA events and a monthly newsletter, the Adjutant's Journal. And, if you are a member reenactor you can participate in our living history and reenactments at the fort.

Fill out the application form today!
